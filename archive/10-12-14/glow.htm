GLOW(2010)
GLOW Festival logo rebrand and event poster. The event poster is composed of multiple layers of fluorescent letter-sized fliers with alternating information stacked into rows and columns of packets. Over time viewers who take the fliers will change the coloration of the poster, thus creating a low-tech interactive advertisement for Santa Monica's GLOW Festival.
