Tiburcio Vasquez: Villian/Hero (2011)
A 150-page book exploring Vaquez Rock's namesake: Tiburcio Vasquez. Vasquez Rocks, a Los Angeles county park, is one of the most filmed parks due to it's proximity to Hollywood. It is also the only park in the US named after a bandit; the notorious Tiburcio Vasquez. Though he lived a life of thievery and debauchery, many Mexicans viewed him as a Robin Hood, a social bandit, and a folk hero. This book takes another look into the life of Vasquez and whether or not Vasquez Rocks was named justifiably.
