About
I truly believe that design, technology, social media, as well as pure enthusiasm can repair and improve the human condition.

As a recent graduate from Art Center College of Design with a BFA in graphic design, I developed a strong interest in the relationship between the role of visual graphics in interaction and user experience design.

If I weren't in front of my computer it's most likely that I would be found outside in the sunlight—exploring some trail in a state or national park.
