This project is an activation of preexisting space. A Set of cube forms was constructed and introduced into a series of environmental situations. At their most basic level, the cubes are placeholders of an often overlooked reality, the physical projection of invisible volume—referential of a monolith. The cubes are blank, neutral in character.  They draw the attention of the occupants within the environment, and the use of scale allows for a tangible subdivision of space and creates a relationship wihtin the family of sizes.
