The Thai that Binds: A Family History

Personal Project

This Family History project is currently being developed. The goal of the project is to document the story of how my father's family arrived to the United States during the era of the Cold War (specifically the Vietnam War). Through escape, starvation and death, the story only exists through word of mouth. With this project, I hope to contribute to the knowledge about my family and to add on to the diverse American story.
