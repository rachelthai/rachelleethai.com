NPS Passport (2011)
My NPS Passport project draws inspiration from an already existing novelty passport system that is sold at visitor stations in the United States National Parks.

This project takes this concept and attempts to adapt it into a next generation passport system by incorporating RFID technology in order to allow users to "check-in" at notable locations within a National Park by interfacing with social media accounts.

The introduction of a social media element to the existing NPS Passport concept allows visitors to explore, share, and document their time at the National Parks, thus increasing awareness and usage of the parks by the public.
